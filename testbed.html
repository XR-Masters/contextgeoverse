The Joint Initiative

Three Organizations. One Ecosystem.

This is not a standalone product. It is a formal joint initiative between three organizations at the center of global spatial computing standards.

Open AR Cloud Association

Global non-profit driving open, interoperable AR Cloud technology. Runs the official OSCP (Open Spatial Computing Platform) Testbed Program.

  • Partner organizations span leading tech companies, universities, and standards bodies worldwide
  • Testbed partners gain official recognition within the global spatial computing ecosystem
  • Pathway to Metaverse Standards Forum (MSF) Forum Labs Partnership

XR Masters / MyGeoVerse

Creator of the MyGeoVerse platform — the geospatial layer of AI, enabling spatially and contextually aware experiences across devices. 10+ years building AR/XR with offices in Washington DC, Oslo, and Istanbul.

  • Leading the OGC/OSCP GeoPose Standards Working Group
  • Demonstrated world's first public spatial web implementation of OGC GeoPose + POI standards
  • Provides full curriculum, platform, coaching, and tools

Augmented City

AR Cloud platform enabling city-scale augmented reality experiences. Provides the Visual Positioning System (VPS) infrastructure for 10–20cm precision localization — far beyond standard GPS accuracy.

  • Active in 30+ partner countries across Europe, Americas, Asia, and Middle East
  • SDKs for iOS, Android, and Unity — student work extends to devices they already own
  • Co-demonstrated world's first OGC GeoPose spatial web implementation with MyGeoVerse
The Build Sprint

The 3-Week Hybrid Sprint

Instructor-led sessions (online, recorded for your school's reuse) combined with hands-on build time. Every cohort ships a real, working AR/XR prototype.

01
Foundations & First Build
  • XR & AI awareness: the post-smartphone era
  • MyGeoVerse platform orientation
  • Intro to 3D asset creation with AI (Vibe Coding)
  • First live AR object placed on school grounds
02
Enhance & Iterate
  • Multi-step refinement: assets, VFX, scene quality
  • Localization: GPS, VPS, QR markers on campus
  • Peer critique + instructor feedback sessions
  • Converge on a cohesive experience concept
03
Deploy & Showcase
  • Integrate AI-driven behaviors: guides, wayfinding
  • Package demo-ready prototype + deployment checklist
  • Live showcase session for school stakeholders
  • Pilot documentation for OARC testbed reporting
Sprint Output: every cohort produces a working AR/XR prototype, a demo script, a deployment checklist, and evaluation notes suitable for stakeholder showcases and OARC testbed documentation.
